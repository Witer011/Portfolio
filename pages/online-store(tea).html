Tea is a gentle reminder to slow down and savor the moment

Black Tea

Black tea is generally stronger in flavour than other teas. All five types are made from leaves of the shrub Camellia sinensis, though Camellia taliensis is also, rarely, used

Green Tea

Green tea is tea that has undergone minimal fermentation. Both green and black tea are obtained from the leaves of the same tea bush, but in different ways

Herbal Tea

Unlike true teas, which are brewed from the leaves of the Camellia sinensis plant, herbal teas are made from dried fruits, flowers, spices, or herbs

White Tea

White tea is made from the same plant as Black, Green and Oolong teas, but the leaves treated slightly differently
